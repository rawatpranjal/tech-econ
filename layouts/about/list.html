About

Who made this?

I'm Pranjal Rawat, an economist and engineer with a PhD from Georgetown University. I've worked at Uber, Roblox, and American Express, building causal inference systems, running experiments, and applying ML to business problems.

Why this site?

A directory of packages, datasets, and learning materials for economists in tech. Working code, not academic papers.

Most of what I learned came from business problems, not theory. Academia values rigor and publishing. Industry needs practitioners who ship.

IO demand models rarely get implemented. Price optimization happens through experiments. Causal inference dominates. Models that don't scale don't ship.

Engineering matters: logging, observability, pipelines. Metrics matter: impressions, clicks, retention, conversion, basket size, sessions.

Scale brings new problems: search, recommendation, fairness, privacy. Business models matter: ads, subscriptions, pricing, marketplaces.

New frontiers keep opening. LLMs, agents, generative AI. Economists have to keep up.
